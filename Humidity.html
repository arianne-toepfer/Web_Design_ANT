City Latitude VS Humidity Visualization

Let's review a scatter plot of Latitude versus Humidity

This scatterplot shows that the greater the latitude the greater the humidity. Between 50 to 80 latitude there is a tight cluster of data showing a higher humidity compared to other latitudes. Between 0 to 40 we see a much larger range of humidities form 20% up to 100%.
